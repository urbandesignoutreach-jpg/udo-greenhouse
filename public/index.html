Root Work Discovery

The Greenhouse Discovery Engine is built on four questions — the same four questions that have always lived at the heart of meaningful work. Answer them honestly, in whatever words feel true. There is no right answer. Every seed counts.

1
The thing U do and lose track of time. What lights U up — a job, a craft, a gift, a passion.
2
The gifts people always come to U for without being asked. Your natural genius.
3
The gap you see in your community, your block, your ecosystem that only you can fill.
4
What U would love to offer — for income, for trade, for love. What wants to be harvested.
