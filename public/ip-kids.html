What Is IP Kids?

Intellectual Property Kids

IP Kids is a personalized discovery experience for young people ages 5–17. Every child enters with their interests and walks away knowing their superpower, their pathway, and the real-world people who share their gifts. We don't tell kids what to be. We show them what they already are.

A Note for Parents & Guardians

IP Kids is for young people ages 5–17. Please read and agree before your child begins.

What this tool does: IP Kids asks for your child's first name, age, and personal interests to generate an encouraging, personalized discovery report.

Privacy: Only a first name and interests are used for this session. No data is stored, sold, or shared with any third party.

AI-generated content: This report is created by an AI and is intended to be inspiring and age-appropriate. We encourage parents to review results together with their child.
